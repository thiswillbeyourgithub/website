    • I spend as much attention on each post as you would expect from a human written blog. No "write a post about X", think instead "reformulate the 3rd argument in the style of Y".
      The ideas are authentic, the style isn't. My english is pretty good, for a frenchman. So you don't want to read my original prose anyway, trust me.
      No SEO optimization. I want to convey my thoughts, not increase traffic or steal attention.
      I opted for AI-aided post rather than not putting my thoughts on the internet.
